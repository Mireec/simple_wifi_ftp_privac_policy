Privacy Policy

Simple WiFi FTP may display banner advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob).

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No analytics tools, tracking libraries, or behavioral profiling mechanisms are used.

Advertisements shown in the app may use non-personalized identifiers to display general ads. No user files or sensitive data are accessed or transmitted by the app itself.
